My First Letter to You

Ananya, the day I met you, the world shifted into soft focus. Every moment since has carried the warmth of your smile and the calm of your voice. I promise to cherish the laughter, the quiet, and the unseen tender pauses that make us "us."

Things I Love About You

I love your kindness that wraps around everyone you meet. I love the way you dream with your whole heart. I love the way you say my name—like I belong. I love that we are East and West, always finding our way back to each other.

Our Future Together

I see soft mornings with you, sunsets painted in pastel, and a lifetime of little penguin waddles. I will hold your hand through every season, every song, and every star we wish upon. Our story is a gentle forever, and I’m grateful it’s ours.
